# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze retail sales performance across multiple product categories.

It was built based on **real business requirements from an industry project**, however all data has been **anonymized and modified to ensure confidentiality**, and no sensitive or proprietary information is included.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard was designed to:

* Identify Top and Bottom 5 products by Sales, Profit, and Quantity Sold
* Analyze sales trends over time (daily, monthly, quarterly, annually)
* Understand relationship between Sales and Profit
* Compare performance across different time periods
* Evaluate discount effectiveness across categories
* Track total number of orders and KPIs
* Enable order-level analysis with interactive filters
* Analyze city-wise sales performance

---
